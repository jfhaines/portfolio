My Story

Hi, I'm Joe. I studied a Bachelor of Business Management at the University of Queensland from 2016 to 2019. After graduation, I got a job as a Social Media Coordinator at Entain, working in this position for just under three years. During this period, in my free time, I finally decided to learn how to code, which was an interest that I had had for quite some time. I looked into the various programming languages and the different areas of programming, and I eventually landed on Javascript & web development. First, I began learning HTML, CSS to provide a solid foundation, and then moved onto frontend vanilla JavaScript. Later on, I learned how to create a backend application using NodeJS & the Express framework. Entain recently offered an internal scholarship to be put into Coder Academy's Accelerated Web Development program, and I jumped at the opportunity. And now here I am.
